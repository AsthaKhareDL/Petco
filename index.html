The Best for your Dogs!

At Petco, we are dedicated to the training and placement of exceptional dogs for a greater purpose. Our mission is to enhance the lives of veterans, children, and individuals with diverse needs by providing highly trained service and therapy dogs. We believe in accessibility, which is why we offer our services at low to no cost, made possible through the support of our community and donors. Since 2017, we’ve contributed over seven figures in invaluable services, thanks to the generosity of those who share our vision.
